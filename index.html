Seattle GeoData Explorer

Ready to Explore Seattle’s Open Data Atlas?

This map contest submission is a digital app for exploring Seattle ArcGIS services by category, source, tag and date.

Submission deadline: May 12, 2026 at noon.

Use the catalog to filter by type, category, source, and tags. Add active layers, inspect metadata, and open attribute tables with one click.
